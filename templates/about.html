Our Mission

Joymeter is dedicated to helping individuals rediscover happiness by tracking and reflecting on their joyful moments. We believe that even the smallest moments of joy can have a profound impact on mental well-being.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where positivity and mindfulness are part of everyday life. By encouraging users to focus on the good, we aim to foster a community of gratitude and happiness.
